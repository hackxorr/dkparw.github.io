----MALTEADA DE CHOCOLATE---

Ingredientes
  * 1 oz de jarabe natural.
  * 4 hielos.
  * 2 bolas de helado de chocolate.
  * 1 taza de leche.
  * Media cucharada de café.
  * 5 mililitros de agua natural

DECORACIÓN
  * 1 lata de crema batida.
  * Chispas de chocolate al gusto.

Utenzilios
  * Un vaso de cristal.
  * Una taza extra.
  * Una cuchara.
  * Una taza medidora.
  * Una licuadora.

PREPARACIÓN
  1-En la taza extra diluir el cafe con agua.
  2-En la licuadora vertir el helado, el hielo, el cafe diluido y mientras se va licuando agregar
    poco a poco hasta conseguir la consistencia deseada.
  3-Servir en el vaso de cristal, decoraramos con la crema batida en circulos posteriormente 
    colocamos chispas de chocolate encima y a beber.

----CHOCOLATE GLAZE-----

Ingredients
  * 1 oz of natural syrup.
  * 4 ice cubes.
  * 2 scoops of chocolate ice cream.
  * 1 cup of milk.
  * Half a spoonful of coffee.
  * 5 milliliters of natural water.

DECORATION
  * 1 can of whipped cream.
  * Chocolate chips to taste.

Utenzilios
  * A glass tumbler.
  * An extra cup.
  * One spoon.
  * One measuring cup.
  * A blender.

PREPARATION
  1- In the extra cup dilute the coffee with water.
  2-In the blender pour the ice cream, the ice, the diluted coffee and while it is blending, add little by little until the desired consistency is obtained.
    little by little until the desired consistency is reached.
  3-Serve in a crystal glass, decorate with the whipped cream in circles and then place chocolate chips on top. 
    place chocolate chips on top and drink.
